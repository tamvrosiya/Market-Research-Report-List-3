<p><p>Water recycling systems consist of various types including Reverse Osmosis Systems, Distillation Systems, Disinfection Methods, and Filtration Methods. Reverse Osmosis Systems use a semi-permeable membrane to remove impurities. Distillation Systems involve boiling water and collecting the steam to separate impurities. Disinfection Methods use chemicals or UV radiation to kill harmful microorganisms. Filtration Methods involve passing water through a physical barrier to remove impurities. These systems are essential in conserving and treating water for reuse in various industries and households.</p></p>
