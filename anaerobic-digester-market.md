<p><strong>The Anaerobic Digester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Upflow Anaerobic Sludge Blanket (UASB)</li><li>Internal Circulation Reactor(IC Reactor)</li><li>Expanded Granular Sludge Bed Digestion (EGSB)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anaerobic digester market types mainly include Upflow Anaerobic Sludge Blanket (UASB), Internal Circulation Reactor (IC Reactor), Expanded Granular Sludge Bed Digestion (EGSB), and others. UASB systems utilize a blanket of sludge particles to treat wastewater. IC Reactors involve internal circulation to enhance anaerobic digestion. EGSB systems use a granular sludge bed for more effective digestion. Other types in the market include plug flow reactors, sequencing batch reactors, and fixed film reactors, each offering unique advantages for different applications in wastewater treatment.</p></p>
