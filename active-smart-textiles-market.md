<p><p>Active Smart Textiles offer various types of sensors for different applications. Resistive sensors are sensitive to pressure, capacitive sensors detect changes in electric field, and piezoresistive sensors measure changes in resistance under pressure. Resistive sensors are commonly used in wearables for motion detection, while capacitive sensors are used for touch-sensitive applications. Piezoresistive sensors are ideal for measuring force and pressure, making them suitable for sports performance monitoring and healthcare applications. Each type of sensor provides unique functionality and benefits in the Active Smart Textiles market.</p></p>
