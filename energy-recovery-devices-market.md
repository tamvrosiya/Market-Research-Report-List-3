<p><p>Energy recovery devices are systems that capture and reuse energy that would otherwise be wasted. Various types of energy recovery devices exist, including reverse turbines, Pelton wheels, pressure exchangers, work exchangers, and electric motor drives. Reverse turbines harness energy from flowing fluids, Pelton wheels extract energy from water jets, pressure exchangers recover energy from high-pressure fluids, work exchangers transfer energy through mechanical work, and electric motor drives convert wasted energy from rotating machinery into usable electricity. Each type of device offers unique benefits and applications in energy efficiency.</p></p>
