<p><p>An Endoscopic Balloon Dilator is a medical device used to dilate strictures or narrow areas in the gastrointestinal tract or esophagus. <p><p>Endoscopic Balloon Dilator is divided into two main types: Single Lumen and Double Lumen. Single lumen balloon dilators are equipped with a single lumen for inflating the balloon, while double lumen balloon dilators have two lumens, one for inflating the balloon and the other for passing instruments or fluids. Single lumen balloon dilators are simpler in design and easier to use, while double lumen balloon dilators provide the added functionality of allowing for additional procedures to be performed simultaneously with dilation.</p></p>
