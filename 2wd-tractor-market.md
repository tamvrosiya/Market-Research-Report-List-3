<p><p>A 2WD tractor, or two-wheel drive tractor, is a type of tractor that only powers two of its four wheels. They are more lightweight and compact compared to their 4WD counterparts, making them easier to maneuver in tight spaces and more fuel-efficient.</p><p>The 2WD tractor market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.7% during the forecast period. Additionally, the rising trend of precision farming and mechanization in agriculture is driving the adoption of 2WD tractors.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the 2WD tractor market is the integration of advanced technologies such as GPS guidance systems and telematics.
