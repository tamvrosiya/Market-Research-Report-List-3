<p><p>Industrial fume cupboards are essential for extracting harmful fumes and gases in industries. There are two main types of industrial fume cupboards: ductless fume cupboards and ducted fume cupboards. Ductless fume cupboards use filters to clean the air before recirculating it back into the room, while ducted fume cupboards exhaust the contaminated air outside through a ventilation system. Both types are important for ensuring the safety and health of workers in industrial settings.</p></p>
