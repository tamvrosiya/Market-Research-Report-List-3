<p><p>Electron Capture Detectors (ECD) are used in gas chromatography to detect electron capturing compounds. There are two main types of ECDs in the market - Radioactive Electron Capture Detectors and Non-radioactive Electron Capture Detectors. </p><p>The Radioactive ECDs contain a small amount of radioactive material to ionize compounds, while Non-radioactive ECDs use a radioactive-free method such as a tritium-free source to achieve the same ionization. Both types are highly sensitive and widely used in environmental analysis, pharmaceuticals, and food testing.</p></p>
