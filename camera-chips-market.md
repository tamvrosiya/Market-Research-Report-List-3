<p><ul><li>Master Chip</li><li>Photosensitive Chip</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Camera chips are essential components in digital cameras, with various types catering to different functions. The master chip serves as the central processing unit, controlling the overall operation of the camera. Photosensitive chips, on the other hand, are responsible for capturing light and converting it into electrical signals for image formation. Other types of camera chips may include image processing chips, autofocus chips, or stabilization chips, each playing a specific role in enhancing the camera's performance and image quality.</p></p>
