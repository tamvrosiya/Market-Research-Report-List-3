<p><p>Single-wheel ditch cleaners are machines equipped with one rotating wheel that is used to clean ditches by removing debris and sediment. They are ideal for smaller ditches and are more compact and maneuverable. Double-wheel ditch cleaners, on the other hand, have two rotating wheels which provide more stability and power, allowing them to handle larger and more challenging ditch cleaning tasks. Both types of ditch cleaners cater to different market segments based on the size and complexity of the cleaning job at hand.</p></p>
