<p><p>Technical coil coatings are applied to metal coils to enhance durability, weather resistance, and aesthetic appeal. The main types of coil coatings include Polyester, Acrylic, Epoxy, Plastisols, and Others. </p><p>Polyester coatings are known for their good resistance to UV rays and outdoor exposure. Acrylic coatings offer excellent color retention and resistance to chalking. Epoxy coatings provide high chemical and abrasion resistance. Plastisols are known for their flexibility and durability. Other coil coatings include silicone modified polyester and PVDF, which offer superior weather resistance and durability.</p></p>
