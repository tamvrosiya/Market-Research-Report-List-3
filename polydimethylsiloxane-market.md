<p><p>Polydimethylsiloxane, commonly known as silicone oil, is available in different market types based on their molecular weights. Higher molecular weight polydimethylsiloxane is thicker and more viscous, suitable for applications requiring greater stability and durability. Ultra High molecular weight polydimethylsiloxane has the highest molecular weight, offering exceptional strength and resilience, making it ideal for specialized applications where extreme performance is required.</p></p>
