<p><p>Impact resistant PMMA is available in two main market types: transparent and opaque. Transparent impact resistant PMMA is clear and allows light to pass through, making it ideal for applications where visibility is important, such as in windows, displays, and protective barriers. Opaque impact resistant PMMA, on the other hand, is solid and does not allow light to pass through. It is commonly used in applications where privacy or light-blocking properties are desired, such as in partitions, signage, and packaging.</p></p>
