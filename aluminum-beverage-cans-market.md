<p><p>Aluminum beverage cans come in two main types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans are made by welding together three separate components: the body, the end, and the tab. They are typically used for larger size cans. Two-piece cans, on the other hand, are made from only two components: the body and the end. They are generally used for smaller size cans. Both types of cans are widely used in the packaging of various beverages due to their light weight, durability, and recyclability.</p></p>
