<p><p>Light stabilizers are additives used in various industries to protect materials from degradation due to exposure to light. The main types of light stabilizers include Light blockers, which prevent UV light from reaching the substrate; UV absorbers, which absorb harmful UV radiation; Quenchers, which neutralize free radicals generated by UV exposure; Free radical scavengers, which inhibit chain reactions caused by free radicals; and Other market types, which may include blends of different stabilizers or unique formulations specifically designed for certain applications. Each type of stabilizer offers its own benefits in terms of protecting materials from degradation caused by light exposure.</p></p>
