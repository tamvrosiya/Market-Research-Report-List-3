<p><p>Airborne optoelectronic platforms are used for various applications such as surveillance, reconnaissance, and environmental monitoring. They can be classified into two main types: multispectral and hyperspectral. Multispectral platforms capture images in multiple narrow bands of the electromagnetic spectrum, allowing for basic differentiation between objects. Hyperspectral platforms, on the other hand, capture images in hundreds of narrow bands, providing detailed information about the composition of objects. Both types of platforms play a vital role in remote sensing and data collection for a wide range of industries.</p></p>
