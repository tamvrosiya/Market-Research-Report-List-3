<p><p>The vehicle differential market consists of three main types: Open Differential, Limited-Slip Differential, and Locking Differential. Open differentials are the most common type, allowing the wheels to spin at different speeds for smooth turning. Limited-slip differentials provide better traction by transferring power to the wheel with more grip. Locking differentials lock the wheels together for maximum traction in off-road or low-traction situations. Each type of differential offers unique benefits for different driving conditions and preferences.</p></p>
