<p><p>Vehicle Leveling System Market is segmented into Electric Leveling System and Hydraulic Leveling System. Electric Leveling System operates by using electric motors to raise or lower the vehicle to ensure it stays level on uneven surfaces. On the other hand, Hydraulic Leveling System uses hydraulic pumps and cylinders to adjust the vehicle's height for leveling. Both systems play a crucial role in enhancing stability and safety while driving, especially for large vehicles such as RVs and trucks.</p></p>
