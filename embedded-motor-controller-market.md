Servo embedded motor controllers are widely used in applications needing accurate position control such as robotics and CNC machines. On the other hand, stepper embedded motor controllers are commonly employed in applications requiring precise speed and torque control without the need for position feedback, like in 3D printers and automated equipment. Both types play a significant role in ensuring smooth and efficient operation of machinery and equipment.</p></p>
