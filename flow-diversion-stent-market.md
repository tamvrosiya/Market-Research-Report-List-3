<p><p>Flow Diversion Stent Market Types include Single Channel and Dual Channel options. Single Channel stents have a single lumen to divert blood flow from an aneurysm, while Dual Channel stents have two separate channels for better control of flow diversion. Single Channel stents are more commonly used for simpler aneurysms, while Dual Channel stents are preferred for more complex cases where precise flow control is needed. Both types of stents are important tools in the treatment of cerebral aneurysms, offering different benefits depending on the specific patient needs.</p></p>
