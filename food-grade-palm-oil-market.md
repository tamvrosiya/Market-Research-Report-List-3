<p><p>Food Grade Palm Oil Market includes two main types: Crude Palm Oil (CPO) and Palm Olein. Crude Palm Oil is the basic form of palm oil obtained directly from the palm fruit, while Palm Olein is a refined, liquid fraction of CPO that is used for cooking purposes due to its high stability at high temperatures. CPO is commonly used in food processing industries for the production of various food products, while Palm Olein is preferred by households for frying and cooking purposes.</p></p>
