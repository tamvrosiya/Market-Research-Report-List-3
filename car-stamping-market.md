<p><p>Car stamping is a process used in the automotive industry to shape metal sheets into various parts of a vehicle. Hot stamping involves heating the metal sheet before stamping to increase its formability and strength. Cold stamping, on the other hand, does not involve heating the metal sheet and is generally used for less complex components. Both types of stamping have their own advantages and are used based on the specific requirements of the part being produced.</p></p>
