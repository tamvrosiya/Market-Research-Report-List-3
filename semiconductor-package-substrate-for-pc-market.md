<p><p>Semiconductor package substrates for PC market includes different types such as FC-BGA (Flip Chip-Ball Grid Array), FC-CSP (Flip Chip-Chip Scale Package), WB BGA (Wire Bond-Ball Grid Array), and WB CSP (Wire Bond-Chip Scale Package). FC-BGA and FC-CSP are flip chip packages that offer high performance and reliability. WB BGA and WB CSP are wire bond packages that are cost-effective and widely used in consumer electronics. Each type caters to different requirements of PC manufacturers in terms of performance, cost, and reliability.</p></p>
