<p><p>Micro irrigation systems consist of various types such as drip irrigation, micro sprinkler irrigation, bubbler irrigation, and others. Drip irrigation involves delivering water directly to the roots of plants, while micro sprinkler irrigation uses small sprinklers to evenly distribute water. Bubbler irrigation releases water in a controlled manner, ideal for larger plants. Other types of micro irrigation systems may include misting systems or irrigation systems specifically designed for hanging baskets. Each type is designed to efficiently deliver water to plants while conserving resources.</p></p>
