<p><p>Steel slag market can be categorized into three types - Blast Furnace Slag, Steelmaking Slag, and Others. Blast Furnace Slag is produced during the iron-making process and is a byproduct of blast furnaces. Steelmaking Slag is generated during the steelmaking process and consists of impurities removed from the steel. The Others category includes slags from sources such as electric arc furnaces and ladle furnace. Each type of slag has its unique characteristics and applications in various industries.</p></p>
