<p><p>The oil immersed transformer market consists of two main types of transformer oils: paraffin based transformer oil and naphtha based transformer oil. Paraffin based transformer oil is a type of mineral oil that provides good dielectric strength, thermal stability, and oxidation resistance. On the other hand, naphtha based transformer oil is derived from the distillation of crude oil and offers enhanced cooling properties and higher flash point. Both types of transformer oil are commonly used in oil immersed transformers to provide insulation and cooling for efficient operation.</p></p>
