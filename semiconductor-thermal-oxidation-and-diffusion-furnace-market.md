Horizontal furnaces are typically used for batch processing, allowing multiple wafers to be heated and treated simultaneously. Vertical furnaces, on the other hand, are more commonly used for single wafer processing, providing better control over the oxidation and diffusion processes. Both types of furnaces play a critical role in the semiconductor industry, facilitating the production of high-quality semiconductor devices.</p></p>
