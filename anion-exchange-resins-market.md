<p><p>Anion-exchange resins can be categorized into two main types: Acid Resins and Alkali Resins. Acid resins are designed to exchange anions such as chloride, nitrate, and sulfate with other anions, while Alkali resins are used to remove anions like bicarbonate, carbonate, and hydroxide. Both types of resins are widely used in industries such as water treatment, pharmaceuticals, and food processing to remove impurities and improve overall product quality. These resins play a crucial role in various purification processes.</p></p>
