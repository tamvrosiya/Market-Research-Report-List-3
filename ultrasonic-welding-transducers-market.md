<p><p>Ultrasonic welding transducers can be classified into three main types based on their vibration direction: bottom vibration, side vibration, and top vibration. Bottom vibration transducers are attached to the bottom of the welding horn, side vibration transducers are attached to the side, and top vibration transducers are attached to the top. Each type has its own advantages and applications in ultrasonic welding processes, depending on factors such as the materials being welded and the desired welding quality.</p></p>
