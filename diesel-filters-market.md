<p><p>Diesel filters are essential components in vehicles and machinery to prevent contaminants from entering the engine. The market for diesel filters includes two main types: in-line type filters and built-in type filters. In-line type filters are installed outside of the engine system and can be easily replaced when needed. Built-in type filters are integrated within the engine system and require less maintenance. Both types offer effective filtration of impurities and help to improve the overall performance and longevity of diesel engines.</p></p>
