<p><p>Portable humidifiers are devices that add moisture to the air in a room. There are two main types of portable humidifiers: warm mist humidifiers and cool mist humidifiers. Warm mist humidifiers work by heating water to create steam, while cool mist humidifiers use a fan to evaporate water into the air. Warm mist humidifiers are often used for relieving congestion and cold symptoms, while cool mist humidifiers are preferred for adding moisture to dry indoor air without the risk of burns. Both types are popular choices for improving air quality and comfort in homes and offices.</p></p>
