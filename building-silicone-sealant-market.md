<p><p>Building silicone sealants are classified into two main types based on the number of components they contain: one-component and two-component sealants. One-component sealants are pre-mixed and ready to use, offering ease of application for simple sealing tasks. Two-component sealants require mixing of two separate components before application, providing a more durable and effective sealing solution for more complex applications. Both types offer different advantages and are suitable for various sealing needs in the construction industry.</p></p>
