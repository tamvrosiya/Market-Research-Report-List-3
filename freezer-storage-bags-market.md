<p><p>Freezer storage bags are available in various types including PE (polyethylene), PVC (polyvinyl chloride), and PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride). PE bags are known for their flexibility and durability, making them suitable for storing a wide range of food items in the freezer. PVC bags are known for their transparency and resistance to moisture, while PVDC bags have superior barrier properties, making them ideal for preventing freezer burn. Each type of bag offers unique features and benefits to meet different storage needs.</p></p>
