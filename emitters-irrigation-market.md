<p><p>Emitters irrigation market includes two main types: Drip Irrigation and Micro-irrigation. Drip Irrigation involves delivering water directly to the roots of plants through a network of tubes and emitters, reducing water usage and improving plant health. Micro-irrigation, on the other hand, is a type of drip irrigation that involves using small emitters to provide a slow, steady flow of water to plants. Both types of emitters irrigation systems are effective in conserving water and improving crop yields.</p></p>
