<p><strong>The Shower Drains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point Drains</li><li>Linear Drains</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shower drains come in two main types: Point Drains and Linear Drains. Point Drains are located in the center of the shower floor and funnel water directly into the drain. These are traditional and cost-effective. On the other hand, Linear Drains are long and narrow drains placed along one side of the shower. They provide a more modern and stylish look to the shower, as well as better drainage efficiency. Both types offer unique benefits and cater to different aesthetic preferences in the market.</p></p>
