<p><ul><li>Single Use</li><li>Reusable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rapid infusers are available in two main market types: Single Use and Reusable. Single Use rapid infusers are designed for one-time use and are disposed of after each use, ensuring sterility and eliminating the need for cleaning and maintenance. On the other hand, Reusable rapid infusers can be cleaned, sterilized, and used multiple times, making them a cost-effective option for healthcare facilities with high patient volumes. Both types provide fast and efficient fluid delivery during emergency situations or surgical procedures.</p></p>
