<p><strong>The Residential Lighting Fixtures Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incandescent</li><li>Fluorescent</li><li>LED & OLED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Residential lighting fixtures market is categorized into four main types: incandescent, fluorescent, LED, and OLED. Incandescent fixtures use a wire filament heated to a high temperature to produce light, but are less efficient. Fluorescent fixtures use gas and a phosphor coating to produce light, with better efficiency but can be harsh. LED fixtures use light-emitting diodes for efficient and long-lasting light. OLED fixtures use organic compounds to produce light, offering unique design possibilities but are still emerging in the market.</p></p>
