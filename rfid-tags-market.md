<p><strong>The RFID Tags Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID tags can be categorized into two main types: Active and Passive. Active RFID tags have a power source and can transmit signals over longer distances, making them ideal for tracking high-value assets or vehicles. Passive RFID tags do not have a power source and instead rely on the reader's signal to transmit data, making them more cost-effective and suitable for tracking inventory or retail items. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various industries for tracking and identification purposes.</p></p>
