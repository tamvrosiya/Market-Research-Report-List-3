<p><strong>The Specialty Surfactants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anionic Surfactant</li><li>Cationic Surfactant</li><li>Amphoteric Surfactant</li><li>Nonionic Surfactant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Specialty surfactants are categorized into four types based on their chemical composition and properties:</p><p>1. Anionic surfactants are negatively charged and commonly used in household cleaners and personal care products.</p><p>2. Cationic surfactants are positively charged and are often found in fabric softeners and hair care products.</p><p>3. Amphoteric surfactants contain both positive and negative charges, making them versatile and suitable for a wide range of applications.</p><p>4. Nonionic surfactants do not carry a charge and are gentle on the skin, making them ideal for use in pharmaceuticals and cosmetics.</p></p>
