<p><ul><li>DEXA Technology</li><li>NIR Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Meat Fat Analyzer market includes two main types of technologies: DEXA Technology and NIR Technology. DEXA Technology, also known as Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry, uses X-ray technology to accurately measure the fat content in meat. On the other hand, NIR Technology, or Near-Infrared Spectroscopy, utilizes light to analyze the composition of meat, including fat content. Both technologies are widely used in the food industry to ensure quality control and accurate labeling of meat products.</p></p>
