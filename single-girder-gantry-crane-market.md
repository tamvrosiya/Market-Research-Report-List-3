<p><p>Single Girder Gantry Crane Market includes two main types: Gantry Crane and Half-Gantry Crane. Gantry Cranes are equipped with two legs on either side of the bridge beam, providing stability and support for heavy lifting operations. On the other hand, Half-Gantry Cranes only have one supporting leg on the side facing the load, making them more suitable for applications where space is limited. Both types of cranes are commonly used in industries such as shipping yards, construction sites, and manufacturing facilities for their versatility and efficiency in material handling.</p></p>
