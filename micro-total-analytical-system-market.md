<p><p>Micro total Analytical System (µTAS) market includes different types of chips such as CD-ROM Chip and EFAD Chip. CD-ROM Chip is a compact disc read-only memory chip used for storing and retrieving information. On the other hand, EFAD Chip is an electrically programmable analog device chip used for signal processing. Both these chips are essential components in µTAS for performing various analytical tasks and processes in a miniaturized system. They play a significant role in improving efficiency and portability of analytical devices.</p></p>
