<p><p>Polarization Maintaining Optical Circulators are devices used in fiber optic communication systems to control the direction of light waves based on their polarization. They come in two main types: Three Port and Quad Port. Three Port circulators have one input and two output ports, enabling efficient routing of polarized light. On the other hand, Quad Port circulators have one input and three output ports, providing additional flexibility in polarization management. Both types are crucial in ensuring signal integrity and reducing signal loss in high-speed optical networks.</p></p>
