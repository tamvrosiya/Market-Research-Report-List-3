<p><p>Micro Annular Gear Pump is a type of pump that utilizes a rotating gear within a circular chamber to move fluids. Two main types are External Gear Pump and Internal Gear Pump. External Gear Pump has gears located outside the main pumping chamber, providing high pressure and efficiency. Internal Gear Pump has gears within the pumping chamber, allowing for better suction and handling of low viscosity fluids. Both types are commonly used in industries such as automotive, medical devices, and aerospace for precise fluid handling applications.</p></p>
