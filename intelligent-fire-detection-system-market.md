<p><p>Intelligent fire detection systems include addressable and conventional fire detection systems. Addressable systems allow for each device to have a unique address, providing specific information about the location of a fire or fault. Conventional systems divide a building into zones with multiple devices connected to each zone, indicating a general area of concern. Both types of systems play a crucial role in ensuring early detection of fires, minimizing damage, and ensuring the safety of occupants in buildings.</p></p>
