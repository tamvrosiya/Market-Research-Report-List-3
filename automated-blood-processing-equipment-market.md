<p><p>Automated blood processing equipment includes automatic blood separators and automatic blood processing systems. Automatic blood separators are used to separate blood components, such as red cells, platelets, and plasma, for transfusion purposes. They streamline the process of blood component separation and ensure efficiency in blood banks and healthcare facilities. Automatic blood processing systems, on the other hand, automate the entire blood processing workflow, from sample preparation to final storage. These systems improve accuracy, reduce processing times, and enhance the overall quality of blood processing operations in healthcare settings.</p></p>
