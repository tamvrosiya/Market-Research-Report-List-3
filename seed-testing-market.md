Purity Test determines the level of pure seeds in a sample. Viability Test checks the ability of seeds to germinate under suitable conditions. Germination Test assesses the percentage of seeds that are capable of sprouting. These tests play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and performance of seeds in agriculture and horticulture.</p></p>
