<p><p>Small tower crane market types include self-erecting tower cranes, flat top tower cranes, hammerhead tower cranes, and luffing jib tower cranes. Self-erecting tower cranes are easy to set up and dismantle, making them ideal for smaller construction projects. Flat top tower cranes have a flat top without a trolley, allowing for easier transport and assembly. Hammerhead tower cranes have a fixed horizontal jib, providing high lifting capacities. Luffing jib tower cranes have a flexible jib that can be adjusted for projects with limited space or obstacles.</p></p>
