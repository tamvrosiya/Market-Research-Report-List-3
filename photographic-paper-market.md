<p><ul><li>Silver Halide Photographic Paper</li><li>Inkjet Photographic Paper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Photographic paper market can be divided into two main types - Silver Halide Photographic Paper and Inkjet Photographic Paper. Silver Halide Photographic Paper is traditional photo paper that uses a light-sensitive emulsion containing silver salts to produce high-quality prints. On the other hand, Inkjet Photographic Paper is designed for use with inkjet printers, utilizing special coatings to absorb ink and deliver vibrant, sharp images. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of photographers and consumers in the market.</p></p>
