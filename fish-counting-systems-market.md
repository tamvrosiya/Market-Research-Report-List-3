<p><p>Fish counting systems have two main market types: stationary and mobile. Stationary systems are fixed in place and typically used in aquaculture facilities or research centers. They provide continuous monitoring of fish populations in a specific location. On the other hand, mobile systems are portable and can be easily moved to different locations for on-the-go fish counting. These systems are commonly used in field studies, fisheries management, and environmental monitoring. Both stationary and mobile systems play a crucial role in accurately assessing fish populations and making informed decisions about their management.</p></p>
