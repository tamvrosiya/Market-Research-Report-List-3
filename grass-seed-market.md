<p><ul><li>Cool Season Grass</li><li>Warm Season Grass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Grass seed markets are generally categorized into two main types: Cool Season Grass and Warm Season Grass. Cool Season Grasses, such as fescue and perennial ryegrass, thrive in cooler climates and are commonly found in regions with harsh winters. Warm Season Grasses, including Bermuda and Zoysia grass, are better suited for hot and humid climates. Each market type caters to specific environmental conditions and is chosen based on the climate of the area where the grass will be planted.</p></p>
