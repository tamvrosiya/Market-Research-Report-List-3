<p><p>Emulsion PVC Paste Market Types refer to two production methods: Micro Suspension Method and Emulsion Method. The Micro Suspension Method involves dispersing PVC resins in water using a stabilizer, resulting in a fine suspension of particles. On the other hand, the Emulsion Method involves polymerizing vinyl chloride monomers in water to form PVC particles. Both methods are widely used in the market for manufacturing various PVC products, with each method offering its own advantages and applications.</p></p>
