<p><p>Medium pressure relief valves are classified into two main types: spring-type and lever-type. Spring-type relief valves rely on the force of a spring to control the pressure inside a system by opening and closing automatically. On the other hand, lever-type relief valves are manually operated and controlled by a lever mechanism. Both types are commonly used in various industries to ensure the safe operation of equipment and prevent damage from excessive pressure buildup. These valves play a critical role in maintaining system efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
