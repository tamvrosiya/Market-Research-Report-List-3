<p><strong>The Carpal Tunnel Release Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open CTR Systems</li><li>Endoscopic CTR Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carpal Tunnel Release (CTR) Systems market consists of two main types: Open CTR Systems and Endoscopic CTR Systems. Open CTR Systems involve making a small incision in the wrist to access and release the carpal tunnel ligament. On the other hand, Endoscopic CTR Systems utilize a miniaturized camera to perform the procedure through smaller incisions, offering less scarring and quicker recovery. Both types of systems aim to alleviate the symptoms of carpal tunnel syndrome by relieving pressure on the median nerve in the wrist.</p></p>
