<p><ul><li>Steam Dried(SD) Fish Powder</li><li>Flame Dried(FD) Fish Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fish powder market can be categorized into two types - Steam Dried (SD) Fish Powder and Flame Dried (FD) Fish Powder. SD Fish Powder is produced by drying fish using steam, which helps retain the natural flavor and nutrients of the fish. On the other hand, FD Fish Powder is produced by drying fish using high temperatures, resulting in a more concentrated flavor. Both types of fish powder are widely used in various food products and animal feed due to their high protein content and versatility.</p></p>
