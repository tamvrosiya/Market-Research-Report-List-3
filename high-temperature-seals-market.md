<p><p>High temperature seals are used in various industrial applications to prevent leakage and maintain operational efficiency in extreme temperature conditions. The market for high temperature seals can be categorized into three types - O-rings, gaskets, and other sealing products. O-rings are circular seals typically used in rotating or reciprocating motion applications. Gaskets are flat seals used to prevent leakage between two surfaces. Other sealing products include diaphragms, packings, and lip seals, catering to specific sealing requirements in high temperature environments.</p></p>
