<p><p>Apricot oil is extracted in two main ways: solvent extraction method and cold pressed method. The solvent extraction method involves using chemicals to pull the oil from the apricot kernel, resulting in a higher yield but potentially lower quality oil. The cold pressed method, on the other hand, involves pressing the kernels to extract the oil without the use of chemicals or heat, resulting in a purer, more natural oil. Both methods have their own market types catering to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
