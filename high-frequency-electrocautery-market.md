<p><ul><li>Monopolar Circuit</li><li>Bipolar Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Frequency Electrocautery Market has two main types of circuits, Monopolar and Bipolar. In Monopolar Circuit, the electrical current flows from the generator through a single electrode to the patient's body, where it produces heat and cuts or coagulates tissues. In Bipolar Circuit, the electrical current flows between two closely spaced electrodes, minimizing the risk of damage to surrounding tissues. Both circuits have their own applications and advantages, catering to different surgical needs and preferences in the medical field.</p></p>
