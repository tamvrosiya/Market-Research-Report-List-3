<p><ul><li>Single-Effect Rising Film Evaporators</li><li>Multi-Effect Rising Film Evaporators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rising Film Evaporators (RFE) are widely used in various industries for the concentration of liquid products. Single-Effect RFEs operate by heating the liquid feed at the bottom of the evaporator, causing it to rise in a thin film along the heating surface. Multi-Effect RFEs utilize multiple evaporator stages to further increase efficiency and reduce energy consumption. Both types offer benefits such as high heat transfer rates and low fouling tendencies, making them popular choices for liquid concentration processes.</p></p>
