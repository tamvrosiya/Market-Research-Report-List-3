<p><strong>The Area CMOS Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Side Illuminated Area CMOS Image Sensors</li><li>Back Side Illuminated Area CMOS Image Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Front Side Illuminated Area CMOS Image Sensors are traditional image sensors where the light enters through the front side, allowing for efficient photon capture but with limited ability to capture light at longer wavelengths. Back Side Illuminated Area CMOS Image Sensors have the light-sensitive area on the back side, allowing for better sensitivity to longer wavelengths and improved performance in low light conditions. Both market types cater to various applications such as smartphones, digital cameras, automotive cameras, and medical imaging devices.</p></p>
