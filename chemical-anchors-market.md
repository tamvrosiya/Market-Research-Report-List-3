<p><p>Chemical anchors are industrial adhesives used to bond or anchor materials together. The market for chemical anchors is divided into two main types: Injectable Adhesive Anchors and Capsule Adhesive Anchors. Injectable adhesive anchors are in the form of a liquid that is injected into pre-drilled holes, while capsule adhesive anchors come in the form of a sealed capsule that is ruptured and mixed before application. Both types provide strong and durable bonding for materials in construction and industrial applications.</p></p>
