<p><p>Common Mode Inductors are widely used in electronic devices to suppress electromagnetic interference. There are two main market types for Common Mode Inductors: SMD Common Mode Choke Coils and Through Hole Common Mode Choke Coils. SMD Common Mode Choke Coils are surface mount devices that are more suitable for compact designs and automated assembly processes. Through Hole Common Mode Choke Coils are mounted through holes on PCBs and are preferred for applications with higher current and power requirements. Both types are essential for ensuring signal integrity and mitigating noise in electronic circuits.</p></p>
