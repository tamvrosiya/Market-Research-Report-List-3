<p><strong>The Glass Cleaning Robot Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drag-wiper</li><li>Roller-wiper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass cleaning robot market can be divided into two main types based on the cleaning mechanism: Drag-wiper and Roller-wiper. Drag-wiper robots use a flat cleaning pad that is dragged across the surface of the glass to remove dirt and smudges. Roller-wiper robots, on the other hand, use a rotating roller that moves up and down the glass to clean it thoroughly. Both types of robots are popular choices for cleaning windows and other glass surfaces efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
