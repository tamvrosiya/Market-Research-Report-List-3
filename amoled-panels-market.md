<p><p>AMOLED panels are divided into two main market types - Flexible AMOLED Panels and Rigid AMOLED Panels. Flexible AMOLED panels are designed to be bendable and durable, making them suitable for use in curved or foldable displays. On the other hand, Rigid AMOLED panels have a fixed shape and are commonly found in flat-screen displays such as smartphones and TVs. Both types of panels offer high-quality display resolutions and vibrant colors, catering to different consumer needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
