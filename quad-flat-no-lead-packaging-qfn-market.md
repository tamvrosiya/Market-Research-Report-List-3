<p><p>Quad-Flat-No-Lead Packaging (QFN) is a type of surface-mount packaging used for integrated circuits. This method helps reduce costs and improve lead consistency. Sawn Type involves cutting the leads using a sawing process, providing more flexibility in lead configuration. Both types offer advantages in terms of cost-effectiveness and functionality, catering to different requirements in the semiconductor and electronics industries.</p></p>
