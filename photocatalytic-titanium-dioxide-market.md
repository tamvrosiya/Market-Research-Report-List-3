<p><p>Photocatalytic Titanium Dioxide market can be categorized into two main types: powder and colloidal. Powder type titanium dioxide is in a solid form and is commonly used in applications such as paints, coatings, and construction materials. On the other hand, colloidal titanium dioxide consists of particles suspended in a liquid, making it ideal for applications such as water treatment and air purification. Both types of titanium dioxide exhibit photocatalytic properties, which make them effective in breaking down pollutants and improving air and water quality.</p></p>
