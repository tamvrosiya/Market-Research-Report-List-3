<p><p>The Heavy Duty Damper market offers two main types: Hydraulic and Pneumatic. Hydraulic dampers use fluid to control movement, providing smooth and precise damping in heavy-duty applications. Pneumatic dampers, on the other hand, use air pressure to absorb energy and reduce vibrations. Both types are commonly used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and industrial machinery to provide stability and control in various operating conditions. Customers can choose between hydraulic and pneumatic dampers based on their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
