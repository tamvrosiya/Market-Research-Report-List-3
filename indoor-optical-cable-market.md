Single Core cables consist of a single optical fiber, while Double Core cables have two optical fibers within the same cable. Multi Core cables contain multiple optical fibers, typically ranging from 4 to 24 fibers. Each type of cable offers different capacities and performance levels, catering to diverse indoor optical communication needs.</p></p>
