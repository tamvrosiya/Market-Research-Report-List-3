<p><p>Automatic ticket gates are available in various types to accommodate different space and security needs. Swing gates operate by swinging open when a valid ticket is presented; full panel gates consist of glass or metal panels that slide open; tripod turnstiles feature three arms that rotate to allow entry; and bi-parting gates open by splitting in the middle. Each type serves a specific purpose and can be customized based on the requirements of the location they are installed in.</p></p>
