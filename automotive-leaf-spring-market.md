<p><ul><li>Double End</li><li>Open End</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive leaf springs are classified into two main types: double end and open end. In a double end leaf spring market, both ends of the spring are secured to the vehicle chassis, providing better stability and control. On the other hand, in an open end leaf spring market, one end of the spring is attached to the vehicle chassis while the other end is left free, allowing for more flexibility and a smoother ride. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the vehicle.</p></p>
