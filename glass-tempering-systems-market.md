<p><p>Glass tempering systems come in various types to suit different manufacturing needs. Horizontal systems are used for large-scale production, while vertical systems are ideal for smaller spaces. Flat systems are used for standard glass shapes, while continuous systems are best for high-volume production. Oscillating systems provide flexibility in terms of glass thickness and size. Bent systems are designed for curved glass products. Uni-directional systems temper glass in one direction, while bi-directional systems temper glass in both directions for added strength and durability.</p></p>
