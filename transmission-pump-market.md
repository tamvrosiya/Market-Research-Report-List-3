<p><strong>The Transmission Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed displacement pump</li><li>Variable displacement pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transmission pumps are essential components in automotive systems, providing the necessary fluid pressure for proper gear shifting and smooth operation. Fixed displacement pumps deliver a constant flow of fluid, suitable for simpler systems with consistent requirements. Variable displacement pumps offer adjustable flow rates, ideal for more complex systems that need flexibility in fluid delivery. Both types cater to different needs in the transmission pump market, with fixed displacement pumps offering reliability and efficiency, while variable displacement pumps offer adaptability and precision.</p></p>
