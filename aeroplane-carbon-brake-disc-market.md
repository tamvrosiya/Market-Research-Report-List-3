<p><p>The aeroplane carbon brake disc market consists of three main types: Single Disc Brakes, Dual Disc Brakes, and Multiple Disc Brakes. Single Disc Brakes feature one carbon brake disc per wheel, offering simplicity and lower weight. Dual Disc Brakes utilize two discs per wheel, providing increased braking power and efficiency. Multiple Disc Brakes utilize multiple smaller discs in a pack configuration, offering higher thermal capacity and improved braking performance.
