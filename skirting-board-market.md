<p><p>Skirting boards are available in various types such as MDF, hardwood, softwood, polymer, and others. MDF skirting boards are made from medium-density fiberboard, offering a cost-effective and versatile option. Hardwood skirting boards provide a luxurious and durable choice for high-end projects. Softwood skirting boards are a budget-friendly option that is easy to work with. Polymer skirting boards are known for their moisture-resistant properties, making them ideal for kitchens and bathrooms.
