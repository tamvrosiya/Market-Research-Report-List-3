<p><p>Sand paper comes in two main market types: Dry-SP and Wet-SP. Dry-SP is used for sanding materials that do not require water during the sanding process, such as wood or metal. Wet-SP, on the other hand, is designed for materials that need to be wet during sanding, like glass or ceramics. Both types of sand paper are essential tools for smoothing surfaces and preparing them for painting or finishing. Choosing the right type of sand paper is crucial for achieving the desired results in various projects.</p></p>
