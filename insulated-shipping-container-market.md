<p><ul><li>Non-Operational (NOR) Refrigerated Containers</li><li>Insulated Purpose-Built Shipping Containers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Insulated shipping containers come in two main types. Non-Operational (NOR) refrigerated containers are used for transporting goods that require temperature control, but these containers are not actively refrigerated. On the other hand, purpose-built insulated shipping containers are designed specifically for maintaining a stable temperature during transport. These containers are ideal for goods that are sensitive to temperature changes, such as pharmaceuticals or perishable items. Both types of containers play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and safety of goods during transit.</p></p>
