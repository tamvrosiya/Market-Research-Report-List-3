<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic Self Bag Drop</li><li>Hybrid Self Bag Drop</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fully Automatic Self Bag Drop devices are ones that require minimal to no human intervention during the bag drop process. Passengers can simply scan their boarding pass, place their bag on the conveyor belt, and the machine will take care of the rest. Hybrid Self Bag Drop devices, on the other hand, combine both self-service and assisted functions. Passengers can use the machine independently or with the assistance of an attendant if needed. Both types of devices aim to streamline the airport check-in process and improve efficiency.</p></p>
