<p><ul><li>Contact Type</li><li>Non-Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid level switches can be divided into two main types based on their operation: contact type and non-contact type. Contact type switches use physical contact with the liquid to detect the level, while non-contact switches utilize technology such as ultrasonics or optics to sense the level without touching the liquid. Contact type switches are usually more cost-effective and suitable for simple applications, while non-contact switches offer benefits such as precise measurement and compatibility with a wider range of liquids.</p></p>
