<p><p>Powered staplers can be divided into two main market types: disposable and reusable. Disposable powered staplers are designed for one-time use and must be discarded after the staples have been used up. They are typically more cost-effective for users who do not anticipate frequent stapling needs. On the other hand, reusable powered staplers are designed to be reloaded with new staples after each use, making them more sustainable and cost-effective for users who have a higher volume of stapling tasks.</p></p>
