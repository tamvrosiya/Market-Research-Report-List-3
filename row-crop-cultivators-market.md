<p><ul><li>Mounted</li><li>Trailed</li><li>Semi-mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Row crop cultivators are agricultural equipment used for mechanical weed control and soil aeration in row crops such as corn, soybeans, and cotton. These cultivators come in different market types based on their mounting methods. Mounted cultivators are directly attached to the tractor, providing high maneuverability and visibility. Trailed cultivators are towed behind the tractor, offering increased capacity and stability. Semi-mounted cultivators combine features of both mounted and trailed cultivators for optimal balance between maneuverability and capacity. Each market type caters to specific farming needs and conditions.</p></p>
