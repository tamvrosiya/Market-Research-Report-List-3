<p><p>Mechanical Fusible Link is a device designed to break or melt when exposed to high temperatures, triggering a mechanism to shut down a system or activate a safety measure in order to prevent fires or other hazards. Electrical Fusible Link functions similarly to mechanical fusible links, but specifically in electrical systems where breaking the link disconnects power to prevent damage or fires. Both types of fusible links play a crucial role in maintaining safety and preventing accidents in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and industrial manufacturing.</p></p>
