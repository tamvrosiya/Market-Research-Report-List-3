<p><p>Post CMP cleaners are used in the semiconductor industry to remove residue and contaminants left on the wafer surface after the chemical-mechanical polishing process. There are two main types of post CMP cleaners: acidic and alkaline. Acidic cleaners are effective for removing metal oxide residues, while alkaline cleaners are used to remove organic and inorganic contaminants. Both types of cleaners play a critical role in ensuring the cleanliness and performance of the wafer surfaces in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
