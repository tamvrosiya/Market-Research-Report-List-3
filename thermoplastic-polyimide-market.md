<p><ul><li>Extrusion Molding</li><li>Hot Compression Molding</li><li>Direct Forming</li><li>Isotactic Pressing</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyimide market types include various processing methods to shape the material into finished products. Extrusion Molding involves melting the polyimide and forcing it through a die to create a specific shape. Hot Compression Molding uses heat and pressure to mold the polyimide into the desired form. Direct Forming involves shaping the polyimide directly without melting. Isostatic Pressing applies equal pressure from all sides to form the material. Other markets may include 3D printing or injection molding techniques to produce thermoplastic polyimide products.</p></p>
