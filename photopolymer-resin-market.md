<p><p>Photopolymer resin market types include Onium Salts, Organometallic, Pyridinium Salts, Oligomers and Monomers, and Other markets. Onium salts are compounds containing cations and are commonly used as photoinitiators in photopolymer resins. Organometallic compounds contain metal atoms bonded to organic ligands and are utilized in various applications, including as catalysts. Pyridinium salts are often used as stabilizers or additives in photopolymer resins. Oligomers and monomers are building blocks for creating photopolymer resins with specific properties. Other markets may include specialty additives, fillers, or coatings for photopolymer resin formulations.</p></p>
