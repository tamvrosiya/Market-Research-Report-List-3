<p><p>Atomized Copper and Atomized Copper Alloy Powder are categorized based on the method used for their production. Water Atomized powders are created by spraying molten metal into water, resulting in fine particles with a spherical shape. On the other hand, Air Atomized powders are produced by spraying molten metal into a high-pressure gas stream, creating irregularly shaped particles. Both types of powders have unique properties and are used in various applications such as additive manufacturing, metal injection molding, and surface coating.</p></p>
