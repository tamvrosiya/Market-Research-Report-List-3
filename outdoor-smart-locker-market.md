<p><p>Outdoor Smart Locker Market Types can be categorized into Contactless and Contact options. </p><p>Contactless smart lockers operate without physical touch, using technologies such as QR codes or mobile apps for access. This type is ideal for reducing the risk of germ transmission and enhancing user convenience.</p><p>On the other hand, Contact smart lockers require physical interaction, such as using a keypad or key to unlock the locker. While not as sanitary as contactless options, contact smart lockers may be preferred for users who prefer traditional methods of access.</p></p>
