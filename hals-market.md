<p><strong>The HALS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymeric Type</li><li>Monomeric Type</li><li>Oligomeric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HALS market includes three main types: polymeric, monomeric, and oligomeric. Polymeric HALS are incorporated into the polymer matrix during manufacturing, providing long-lasting UV stabilization. Monomeric HALS are reactive additives that migrate to the surface of the polymer to protect against UV degradation. Oligomeric HALS are intermediate in size, providing a balance between ease of handling and efficiency in UV protection. Each type offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications within the polymer industry.</p></p>
