Calcium Silicide Cored Wire (CaSi) is used for deoxidizing and desulfurizing steel. Calcium Solid Cored Wire (Ca) is commonly used for deoxidizing liquid steel. Pure Carbon Cored Wire (C) is utilized for adjusting carbon content in steel. Ferro Calcium Cored Wire (CaFe) is used for improving steel quality. Other types offer specific functionalities for steelmaking processes.</p></p>
