<p><strong>The Piezoresistive Force Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Mode FSR</li><li>Thru Mode FSR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Piezoresistive force sensors operate on the principle of resistance changing with applied force. There are two main types of piezoresistive force sensors - Shunt Mode FSR and Thru Mode FSR. </p><p>Shunt Mode FSRs measure force through changes in resistance along a single path, while Thru Mode FSRs measure force through resistive changes extending through the entire sensor. Shunt Mode FSRs are more sensitive and provide higher resolution, while Thru Mode FSRs offer a larger sensing area and can handle higher loads. Both types are commonly used in various industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
