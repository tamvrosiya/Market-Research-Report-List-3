<p><p>Subminiature basic switches are available in two main types - Open Type and Sealed Type. </p><p>Open Type switches are not enclosed and are exposed to the external environment. They are suitable for applications where dust and liquids are not a concern.</p><p>Sealed Type switches, on the other hand, are encapsulated to prevent ingress of dust and liquids. They are ideal for use in harsh environments where protection from external elements is necessary.</p><p>Both types cater to different market demands based on the level of environmental protection required.</p></p>
