<p><ul><li>Implantable Heart Pump Devices</li><li>Extracorporeal Heart Pump Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centrifugal heart pumps are divided into two main market types: implantable heart pump devices and extracorporeal heart pump devices. Implantable heart pump devices are surgically implanted inside the body to assist with pumping blood in patients with heart failure. On the other hand, extracorporeal heart pump devices are used outside the body to temporarily support heart function during surgeries or for patients waiting for a heart transplant. Both types of centrifugal heart pumps play a crucial role in supporting heart function and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
