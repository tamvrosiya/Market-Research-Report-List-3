<p><ul><li>Inline AOI Equipment</li><li>Offline AOI Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Equipment market consists of two main types: Inline AOI Equipment and Offline AOI Equipment. Inline AOI Equipment is integrated into the production line, allowing for real-time inspection of components during the manufacturing process. This type of equipment helps to identify defects and ensure quality control. On the other hand, Offline AOI Equipment is used separately from the production line for batch inspection of components. It offers flexibility in terms of inspection timing and thoroughness but may not provide immediate feedback on production line performance.</p></p>
