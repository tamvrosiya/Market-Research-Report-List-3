<p><p>Food Grade Lubricants and Greases are classified into three main types: H1, H2, and 3H. H1 lubricants are used in food processing environments where there is potential for incidental food contact. H2 lubricants are used in food processing environments where there is no possibility of contact with food. 3H lubricants are used for equipment that comes in direct contact with food products. Manufacturers typically use H1 and 3H lubricants and greases to meet strict food safety regulations and standards.</p></p>
