<p><strong>The Aircraft Wireless Intercom System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full Duplex Communication</li><li>Semi-duplex Communication</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Wireless Intercom System market can be divided into two types based on communication capabilities: Full Duplex Communication and Semi-duplex Communication. Full Duplex Communication allows users to transmit and receive signals simultaneously, enabling natural two-way conversations without any interruptions. On the other hand, Semi-duplex Communication allows users to either transmit or receive signals at a time, requiring them to take turns speaking and listening. Both types offer communication solutions for aircraft crew members and passengers, with Full Duplex Communication providing a more seamless and efficient communication experience.</p></p>
