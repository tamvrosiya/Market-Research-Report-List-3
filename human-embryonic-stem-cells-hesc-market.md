<p><p>Human embryonic stem cells (HESC) market consists of three main types - totipotent stem cells, pluripotent stem cells, and unipotent stem cells. Totipotent stem cells have the ability to differentiate into any cell type in the body, while pluripotent stem cells can differentiate into many different cell types. Unipotent stem cells, on the other hand, can only differentiate into one specific cell type. Each type of stem cell offers unique potential for regenerative medicine and therapeutic applications in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
