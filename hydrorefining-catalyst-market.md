<p><strong>The Hydrorefining Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-hydrorefining Catalyst</li><li>Hydrorefining Catalyst</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydrorefining Catalysts can be broadly categorized into two types - Pre-hydrorefining Catalysts and Hydrorefining Catalysts. Pre-hydrorefining Catalysts are used in the initial stage of the hydrorefining process to remove impurities and prepare the feedstock for further treatment. Hydrorefining Catalysts, on the other hand, are employed in the main hydrorefining unit to facilitate the conversion of feedstock into high-quality products by catalyzing various chemical reactions. Both types play a crucial role in the hydrorefining process to ensure optimal product quality and process efficiency.</p></p>
