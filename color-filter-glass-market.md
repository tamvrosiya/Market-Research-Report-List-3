<p><strong>The Color Filter Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longpass</li><li>Bandpass</li><li>Shortpass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Color filter glass is used in various applications such as imaging, display, and lighting to manipulate light wavelengths. Longpass filters allow longer wavelengths to pass through, blocking shorter wavelengths. Bandpass filters only allow a specific range of wavelengths to pass through while blocking others. Shortpass filters block longer wavelengths and only allow shorter wavelengths to pass through. These different types of color filter glass cater to different needs in industries such as photography, medical imaging, and spectroscopy.</p></p>
