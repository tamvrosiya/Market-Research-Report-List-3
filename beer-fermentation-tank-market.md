<p><p>Beer fermentation tank market includes conical fermentation tanks and open fermentation tanks. Conical fermentation tanks are the most common type, designed with a cone-shaped bottom to collect yeast and other sediment at the end of the fermentation process. They are more efficient and easier to clean compared to open fermentation tanks, which are shallow vessels without a cone bottom. Open fermentation tanks are traditional and allow for natural yeast and bacteria to ferment the beer, resulting in unique flavors and aromas.</p></p>
