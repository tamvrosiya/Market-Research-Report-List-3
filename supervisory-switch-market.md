<p><p>Supervisory switches are available for different types of valves in the market. For OS&Y valves, supervisory switches are designed to provide monitoring and control functions for open stem and yoke valves. For butterfly valves, supervisory switches are specific to these types of valves to monitor their position and status. Similarly, for post indicator valves, supervisory switches are designed to monitor the operation and position of these valves. Each type of supervisory switch caters to the specific requirements and characteristics of the valve it is intended for.</p></p>
