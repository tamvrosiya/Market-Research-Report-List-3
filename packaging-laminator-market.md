<p><p>Packaging laminator market can be categorized into two main types: Semi-automatic and Fully automatic. In the semi-automatic market, the laminating process requires some manual intervention such as feeding the materials and adjusting settings. On the other hand, in the fully automatic market, the laminating process is completely automated and requires minimal manual intervention. Fully automatic laminators are preferred for high-volume production and offer greater efficiency and convenience. Semi-automatic laminators are suitable for smaller scale operations or for those who require more flexibility in the laminating process.</p></p>
