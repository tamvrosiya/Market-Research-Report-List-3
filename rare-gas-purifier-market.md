<p><p>The Rare Gas Purifier Market includes three main types of tubes: Titanium Tube, Copper Oxide Tube, and Molecular Sieve Tube. Titanium tubes are known for their high strength and resistance to corrosion, making them ideal for purifying rare gases. Copper Oxide tubes are effective at removing trace impurities from gases. Molecular Sieve tubes utilize porous materials to selectively trap and separate gas molecules based on size and polarity. Each type of tube offers unique advantages in purifying rare gases for various industrial applications.</p></p>
