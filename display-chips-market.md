<p><p>Display chips can be categorized into two main types: Independent Display Chips and Integrated Display Chips. Independent Display Chips are standalone chips that are responsible for generating images and graphics for display devices. On the other hand, Integrated Display Chips are integrated into the main processor or graphics processing unit (GPU) and share the same chip package. Integrated Display Chips offer space-saving advantages and can provide better overall performance by utilizing shared resources within the chip package, while Independent Display Chips offer more flexibility and customization options.</p></p>
