Fuel-powered shuttle buses run on traditional gasoline or diesel, offering easy refueling options but contributing to air pollution. Electric-powered shuttle buses use battery technology for zero emissions, making them environmentally friendly but requiring frequent charging. Hybrid-powered shuttle buses combine both fuel and electric power sources for improved efficiency and reduced environmental impact.
