<p><p>Plastic for additive manufacturing refers to the material used in 3D printing. There are three main types: resin-based, powder-based, and filament market. Resin-based materials are liquid polymers that harden when exposed to light. Powder-based materials are small particles that are fused together with a laser or electron beam. Filament market involves feeding plastic filament through a heated nozzle to create layered objects. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications in additive manufacturing.</p></p>
