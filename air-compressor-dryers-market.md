There are two main types of air compressor dryers: refrigerated and regenerative. Refrigerated air compressor dryers use cooling methods to reduce the air temperature and condense moisture, while regenerative air compressor dryers use desiccant materials to absorb moisture. Both types of dryers are widely used in various industries to improve the quality of compressed air and increase the lifespan of equipment.</p></p>
