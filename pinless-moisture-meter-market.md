<p><p>Pinless moisture meters use different technologies to measure the moisture content of various materials. The two main types are Electromagnetic Induction and Sensor Sensing market. Electromagnetic Induction meters use electromagnetic fields to measure the moisture content without penetrating the material. Sensor Sensing meters use sensors to detect changes in electrical resistance caused by moisture. Both types offer non-invasive and accurate moisture measurement solutions for industries such as construction, woodworking, and agriculture.</p></p>
