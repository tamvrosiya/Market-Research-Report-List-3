<p><p>Acoustic wave sensors are used for detecting various physical and chemical properties by utilizing acoustic waves. The market for acoustic wave sensors can be categorized into two main types: Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensors and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) sensors. SAW sensors operate on the surface of a material, while BAW sensors transmit acoustic waves through the bulk of a material. Both types offer unique advantages and applications in industries such as healthcare, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
