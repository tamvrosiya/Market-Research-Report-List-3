<p><p>Waterfree urinal market consists of three main types: Microbiological Systems, Liquid Barrier Systems, and Valve Barrier Systems. Microbiological Systems use bacteria to break down urine, reducing odor and blockages. Liquid Barrier Systems rely on a sealant fluid to prevent odors from escaping and clogs from forming. Valve Barrier Systems use a valve to trap odors and prevent them from entering the restroom. Each system offers unique benefits for water conservation and odor control in public and private restroom facilities.</p></p>
