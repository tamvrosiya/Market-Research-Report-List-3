<p><ul><li>Single Pass</li><li>Dual Pass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bar and plate oil coolers are commonly available in single pass and dual pass configurations. In a single pass design, the oil flows through the cooler only once in a straight line, providing efficient cooling but limited contact time with the cooler. On the other hand, a dual pass design allows the oil to flow through the cooler twice, increasing the contact time and enhancing cooling efficiency. Both types have their own advantages and are used based on specific cooling requirements in various applications.</p></p>
