<p><strong>The Building Plastics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastics</li><li>Thermosetting Plastics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Building plastics market is segmented into two primary types: Thermoplastics and Thermosetting Plastics. Thermoplastics are materials that can be heated and moulded into different shapes multiple times. They are commonly used in construction for applications such as pipes, fittings, and insulation. On the other hand, thermosetting plastics undergo a chemical reaction during curing, resulting in a rigid, durable product that cannot be reshaped. They are often used in building materials such as countertops, flooring, and electrical insulators.</p></p>
