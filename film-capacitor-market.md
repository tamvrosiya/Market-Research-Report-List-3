<p><p>Film capacitors are categorized into two main types: AC film capacitors and DC film capacitors. AC film capacitors are specifically designed for alternating current circuits, offering high insulation resistance and low dielectric absorption. On the other hand, DC film capacitors are optimized for direct current applications, providing stable capacitance values and low energy losses. Both types of film capacitors are widely used in various electronic devices and systems to store and release electrical energy efficiently.</p></p>
