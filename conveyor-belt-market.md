<p><p>Conveyor belts are categorized into three main types: Multiply Fabric Conveyor Belts, Steel Cord Conveyor Belts, and Solid Woven Conveyor Belts. Multiply Fabric Conveyor Belts are made of layers of fabric and rubber for flexibility and strength. Steel Cord Conveyor Belts have steel cords embedded in the rubber for high tensile strength. Solid Woven Conveyor Belts are constructed with a single ply of fabric for durability. Each type of conveyor belt offers unique characteristics suited for different industries and applications.</p></p>
