<p><ul><li>Activated</li><li>Non-activated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum catalysts can be classified into two main types: activated and non-activated. Activated aluminum catalysts are chemically treated to increase their catalytic activity and performance. They are more efficient and effective at promoting chemical reactions. On the other hand, non-activated aluminum catalysts are untreated and have lower catalytic activity. They are simpler and cheaper to produce, but are generally less efficient in catalyzing reactions. The choice between activated and non-activated catalysts depends on the specific requirements of the reaction being catalyzed.</p></p>
