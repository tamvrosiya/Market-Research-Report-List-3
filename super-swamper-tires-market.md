<p><p>Super Swamper Tires come in two main market types: Bias Tire and Radial Tire. Bias Tires are made with layers of cords that crisscross diagonally across the tire, providing better puncture resistance and durability, making them ideal for off-road use. Radial Tires, on the other hand, have cords that run perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better traction and handling on paved roads. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of customers depending on their specific driving conditions and terrain.</p></p>
