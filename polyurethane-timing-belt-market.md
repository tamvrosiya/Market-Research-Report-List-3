<p><strong>The Polyurethane Timing Belt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Trapezoidal Tooth Polyurethane Timing Belt</li><li>Arc Tooth Polyurethane Timing Belt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane timing belts are classified into two main types based on their tooth profiles: Trapezoidal Tooth and Arc Tooth. Trapezoidal Tooth Polyurethane Timing Belts are characterized by their trapezoidal-shaped teeth that provide efficient power transmission in various industrial applications. On the other hand, Arc Tooth Polyurethane Timing Belts have curved teeth that allow for smoother engagement and reduced noise during operation. Both types of belts are widely used in industries such as automotive, manufacturing, and packaging for their high performance and durability.</p></p>
