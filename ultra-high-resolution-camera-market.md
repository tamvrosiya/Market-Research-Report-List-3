<p><strong>The Ultra-High Resolution Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultra-High Resolution Cameras are classified into two main types: Area Scan Camera and Line Scan Camera. Area Scan Cameras capture a two-dimensional image of a scene, making them suitable for applications such as inspection systems and surface defect detection. On the other hand, Line Scan Cameras capture a single line of pixels at a time, and are ideal for applications where continuous inspection of moving objects is required, such as in the printing and packaging industries. Both types of cameras offer superior image quality and precision for various industrial and scientific applications.</p></p>
