<p><p>Human Milk Analyzer market can be categorized into two types: Near-IR Milk Analyzers and Mid-IR Milk Analyzers. Near-IR Milk Analyzers are based on near-infrared spectroscopy technology, which measures the absorption of light by the sample to analyze its composition. On the other hand, Mid-IR Milk Analyzers utilize mid-infrared spectroscopy technology to analyze the composition of the sample by measuring the vibration of the molecular bonds. Both types of analyzers provide accurate and efficient analysis of human milk composition for various applications.</p></p>
