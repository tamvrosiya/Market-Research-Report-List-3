<p><ul><li>LWIR</li><li>MWIR</li><li>SWIR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal scanners are used to detect and measure thermal radiation emitted by an object. There are three main types of thermal scanners based on the wavelength of the radiation they detect - Long-Wave Infrared (LWIR), Medium-Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Short-Wave Infrared (SWIR). LWIR scanners are commonly used for long-range applications, MWIR scanners offer high sensitivity and resolution, and SWIR scanners are suitable for applications requiring a shorter wavelength range. Each type of scanner has its own set of advantages and is used in various industries for different purposes.</p></p>
