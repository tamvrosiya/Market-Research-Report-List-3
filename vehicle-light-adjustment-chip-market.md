<p><p>The vehicle light adjustment chip market is divided into two main types: high beam and low beam. High beam chips are designed to provide maximum visibility at long distances, making them ideal for use in rural areas or on highways. Low beam chips, on the other hand, offer a more focused beam that illuminates the road directly in front of the vehicle, making them better suited for driving in urban areas or on winding roads. Both types of chips play a crucial role in providing safe and efficient lighting for vehicles.</p></p>
